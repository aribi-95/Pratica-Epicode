Abattoir Blues / The Lyre of Orpheus

Nick Cave & The Bad Seeds

Genre: Alternative Rock / Art Rock / Gothic Rock

Duration: 82 minutes

Overview

A double album showcasing the stark duality of Nick Cave’s creative vision: Abattoir Blues roars with urgent, gospel-infused rock, while The Lyre of Orpheus offers a more poetic, introspective counterpart. It's a stunning contrast between fire and reflection, delivered with lyrical mastery and theatrical intensity.
